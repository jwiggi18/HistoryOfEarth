PHYLOTASTIC ED

History of Earth


Through this resource students have the opportunity to explore the relationships between:
Geologic time, Climate, and Biological evolution.

Four videos (Overview, Paleozoic Era, Mesozoic Era, Cenozoic Era), produced by PBSeons, introduce students to the history of earth, hitting the highlights of explosions of diversity and mass extinctions. A link to questions about each video is provided, and self-graded, allowing teachers to ensure students mentally participate in viewing the videos while not adding to the teacher’s grading burden.

After acclimating to geologic time, students can dig deeper, investigating each period through links to videos and reading material.

Using the drop down menus students can learn about Organisms featured in the videos. When A student selects an organism a link is provided to learn more, that organism is highlighted in red on the phylogeny below, and points indicating fossil locations (extinct) or existing populations (extant) at the geologic time of the organism’s existence appear on the map.

Selection of a period of interest from that drop down menu displays earth’s landmasses during the period of interest and highlights that period on the phylogenetic tree.

Adresses Next Generation Science Standards:

  • MS-ESS1-4 Earth's Place in the Universe
  • MS-ESS2-3 Earth's Systems
  • HS-LS4 Biological Evolution: Unity and Diversity
